Geographic Distribution

Single node: no backplane overhead. All media mixed locally on one VM.
Multi-node site: backplane reserves 1 HD per active meeting per transcoding node. Media mixed locally within the site where possible.
Multi-site: backplane overhead scales with site count × meetings. Media traverses inter-site links when conferences span sites.
